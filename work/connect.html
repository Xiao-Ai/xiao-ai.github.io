Connect You is a job hunting and social networking website that provides company referrals for college students and strong candidates for recruiters.

There are two interfaces for students and employers respectively. For students, they could not only get socially connected with each other and also acquire job information on this website. The most exciting part is that students could easily submit their referrals. As for employers, they could post their jobs and view students resumes.

Background Story

I am about to graduate from college and am currently looking for a job. However, finding the first internship or job is hard, especially for a students like me because I do not know people outside of campus. It is frustrating that I sent out 100+ resumes and barely got any phone calls back. I wish I could know more industry professionals to get some interviews.

I felt that there should be more students like me who are having trouble with finding the job. Therefore, I came up with an idea: build a platform for students and make it easier to find jobs.

Interview and Research

To find out the pain point of students when finding the job, and to figure out what kind of employee is the company really want, I interviewed 2 sophomore students who want to find an internship, and 6 graduating students who are currently finding job, and 3 employers. I asked students the problems they have faced and what they need when finding the job. And I also asked employers what kind of staff they are looking for.


"I want to do some internships during summer vacation but don’t know where I can get those information."



—— Wu Yueze, Sophomore Students


"I have sent tons of resumes but haven’t got any phone calls back, not to mention the onsite interview!"



—— Yan Zhilong, Graduating students

"Job experience is prefferd in so many companies, but I just graduated. How can I get the experience when no one wants to hire me in the first place?"



—— Luo Ying, Graduating Students


"Hiring someone who are just graduated is not cost-effective. They don’t have much job experience. It takes time to train them before they could really do something in the job."


—— Conke Hu, CEO of Shanghai MaxWit Software Co. Ltd


"It's not easy to fully evaluate a recent graduated students just from an interview. I need to know them deeper before I could make decision."



—— Celine Sun, HR of Shanghai Wicresoft Co. Ltd.

Implementation

This is my graduation dissertation, so I did all the job, including researching, UX and UI designing and coding. I used front-end framework Bootstrap to make it responsive. Besides, I used Django as back-end framework to make it dynamic and fully functional. Bootstrap and Django were both new to me, but it only took me two days for me to learn Bootstrap and I acquired basic part of Django within one week.
